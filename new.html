Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University


University School of Information, Communication and Technology, located in New Delhi, is one of the premier colleges in the country. Established in 1998, the college is affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU). The college offers full-time undergraduate and postgraduate courses viz. B.Voc, B.Tech and M.Tech dual degree, M.Tech and MCA. Candidates are required to appear for IPUCET examination for admission to B.Voc, B.Tech + M.Tech dual degree as well as MCA, and GATE/CET for M.Tech.
